For Adoption

Beauregard von DufusDifficulty rating: 2 out of 5

I don't know what dufus means but my foster mom says I'm totally one. She calls me Bo-Bo for short except when I'm in trouble, which is not often cause I'm a good guy. I'm house-trained, love other dogs, cats, and kids.

If you throw the ball I will get it over and over. I walk well on a leash, love car rides and swimming in the river. The only thing I don't like is thunderstorms. When there is a storm at night, my job is to lay as close as possible to my foster mom to make sure she doesn't fall asleep while protecting us.

I will bark when somebody new comes to the door but its only because I want them to know I'm here so they will pet me and possibly throw the ball. Also, I come with my own ball throwing stick. Since I've been with my foster mom, I've added cookie monster and couch potato to my resume.

My special ability is that I can carry 3 tennis balls in my mouth.

JenkinsDifficulty rating: 5 out of 5

I'm really really really smart and I don't like laying around all day, so I tend to find things to do. My foster mom says that I need to get a job because all the landscaping is done. She doesn't need any more holes dug under the fence and apparently I've dug up and dragged my last bush into the house.

My foster mom will tell you that I was the one who dismembered the stuff animal collection in the backyard, but she couldn't prove anything. Besides how could I get them when they were staring at me from on top of a 7ft chifferobe.

I love car rides. Often times my foster mom and me will go for a drive at night to relax. When a good song comes on the radio we'll both sing along. Good times.

Speaking of time, I am a great alarm clock. I will wake you up at 6am every morning (plus or minus 1 minute) to go for a run. I don't even worry about whether its a weekend or a holiday or raining. Adopt me and you'll be doing a marathon before you know it.

My special ability is being able to clean the butter dish without moving it on the countertop.

RikerDifficulty rating: 4 out of 5

My previous owner was a Star Trek fan but he got real sick and couldn't look after me anymore. My foster mom says I am a great dog. I'm a real German - German Shepherd, not one of those American - German shepherds with the bad hips.

I do agility and fly ball, and I'm really good at it. So if you like winning - adopt me. I could have gone to police school but my previous owner was afraid I would become mean. And because of cats which are like super tasty tribbles. My foster mom has, on more than one occasion, uttered the words "It's dead Jim". And no - its not impossible. Its quite easy - you just grab them by the neck and snap it.

Kids are a different story. I love kids and I will sleep with them on their bed at night to keep them company. When my buddy's Boy Scout troup has a sleepover in the backyard, I watch out for them all night.

If I get a little peckish I will open the door to the pantry to get a snack. And I've encoded all the doorknobs in the house with doggy braille.

My special ability is that no one will rob your house when I am home.

ChewbaccaDifficulty rating: 1 out of 5

Call me Chewy for short, or Chew-Chew if you like. You might recognize me, I'm Lassie's brother from another mother.

As I get older I am coming to terms with my puppyhood. I grew up resenting my brother's fame. It drove me to a life of drugs and I ended up living on the streets. I was finally arrested in Texas after I got hit by a car. They fixed me up and did a serious deworming. I won't bore you with the details cause my foster mom is not quite over it. Something about not being able to unsee what she saw.

Anyway, here I am. I love women and kids and I will follow you around the house all day, no matter where you go, just to keep you company. I like to sleep in my own bed but it has to be in the same room with you. I don't need to get up early. If you want to go for a walk, I will not pull you even if there are other dogs around. And if you're not feeling up to it, I can watch TV with you instead.

My special ability is being able to give you hugs when your sitting on the toilet no matter what number you're doing.
