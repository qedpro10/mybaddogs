Welcome

Hello and welcome to My Bad Dogs - a website for adopting challenging dogs.

All the dogs on this site are friendly to humans - at least nice humans, and other dogs - at least nice dogs. I can't say the same for other species. Each has many redeeming characteristics and they are all worthy of love, affection and a wonderful home.

Often times people get rid of their dogs because they weren't aware of the breed behaviors of the dog they got. This leads to frustration and ultimately the dog winding up in a shelter. We pair dogs to owners who have experience and understand the needs of specific breeds.

The Adoption Process

The adoption process requires the following steps:

  • Fill out application
  • Phone Screen
  • Home Inspection
  • Dog Meet and Greet

Even if you are not seeing a dog you like right now, you are welcome to fill out the application to start the approval process. The phone screen typically takes 30-45 minutes. If all goes well, the home inspection requires that you set up an appointment with the Humane Society to have one of their members inspect your home to ensure that it is safe and suitable for a dog. Note we don't tell you what we are looking for in the home inspection. Consider it a test.
