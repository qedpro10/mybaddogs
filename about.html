Dog House Rules

  • If the dogs don't like you - it's your fault.
  • Don't worry about taking off your shoes, the dogs don't - worry that is.
  • If you don't want hair on your clothes - stay off the furniture. That's why they call it furniture.
  • It's rude not to throw the ball that has been placed in your lap.
  • If you don't want to be splooged, don't wear shorts.
  • If you don't know what splooging is, come on over... in shorts
  • When going downstairs please hold the handrail. The dogs have no qualms about tripping you to see if you can fall faster than they can run.
